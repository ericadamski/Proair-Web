ABOUT US

PROAIR SDN BHD, a contracting mechanical and electrical company was established in 1990 with the main objective of providing engineering services to the commercial and marine industries. The firm has since successfully completed, in the field of mechanical and electrical engineering works which include hotels, factories, offices etc. Included are many local and foreign vessels (ocean tankers, Offshore Supply Vessels) for the Marine Oil and Gas industry as well as specialized jobs for the defense industry. Proair has completed the HVAC package for Navy ships and is a registered HVAC and Refrigeration contractor with Boustead Naval Dockyard and Malaysian Marine & Heavy Engineering (MMHE).

PROAIR SDN BHD, offers a complete range of engineering services covering among others, investigations, preliminary design and feasibility studies to detailed design, supply and installation, supervision, testing, commissioning and maintenance of HVAC and Refrigeration systems

Backed by various foreign principals from Europe, China and Singapore, the company endeavors to provide efficient services to the client and to improve existing high standard of engineering products and services covering the commercial, industrial, marine and offshore Industries.
