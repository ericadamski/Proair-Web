Managing Director
Mr Kan is the founder and main shareholder of Proair Sdn Bhd. He holds a Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering, and has been trained in system design in air-conditioning (basic & advanced) and computer aided design. Mr Kan has more than 20 years' experience in the heating ventilation and air-conditioning industry.

Ms Shia is responsible for the company’s finance and administration unit of the company. She holds a Masters of Business Administration degree from the University of Bath, United Kingdom and is a member of the Malaysian Institute of Management. Ms Shia has 15 years' experience in the financial services industry prior to joining Proair as the Finance and Administration Director.
